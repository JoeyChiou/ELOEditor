How to Navigate this Content Collection

This content collection was designed to be easy to use and navigated in a manner that suits your learning needs. At the end of this content, a menu will be presented. At any time you may select a topic from the LMS-provided navigation.

Once a topic is selected an internal menu will appear. Clicking the Next button within the content will progress you through the Introduction, JavaScript, API, Data Model Elements and Conclusion content for the selected plug-in. Clicking the Previous button within the content will take you to the previously viewed page. You may also click on the section titles in the internal menu to navigate through the content collection.
